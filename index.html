FAQ
Who needs GST registration?
Any business with annual turnover exceeding ₹40 lakh (₹20 lakh for services) must register for GST. Inter-state suppliers and e-commerce sellers must register regardless of turnover.
How long does GST registration take?
Typically 3–7 working days after document submission. We file the application within 24 hours of receiving all documents.
What if I miss filing deadlines?
Late filing attracts interest (18% p.a.) and late fees (₹50/day, max ₹10,000). We set up reminders to ensure you never miss a deadline.
Can you handle GST notices?
Yes. We draft professional replies to GST notices and represent clients before GST officers. Early action is key — contact us immediately upon receiving any notice.

FAQ
What is the ITR filing deadline?
For individuals (non-audit): July 31st. For businesses requiring audit: October 31st. Belated return can be filed until December 31st with late fees.
Old vs New Tax Regime — which is better?
It depends on your deductions. We analyse both regimes and help you choose the one that results in lower tax liability based on your specific income profile.
Do I need to file if tax is deducted at source?
Yes, if your income exceeds the basic exemption limit (₹2.5L / ₹3L / ₹5L depending on age), filing is mandatory even if TDS is deducted.

FAQ
When are annual ROC filings due?
AOC-4 (Financial Statements) is due within 30 days of AGM; MGT-7 (Annual Return) within 60 days of AGM. Non-compliance attracts heavy penalties.
Is Director KYC mandatory every year?
Yes. Every director with a DIN must file DIR-3 KYC annually by September 30th, or their DIN gets deactivated.
How long does company incorporation take?
Typically 7–15 working days end-to-end, including name approval, MoA/AoA drafting, and Certificate of Incorporation from MCA.

FAQ
When is PF registration mandatory?
Any establishment with 20 or more employees is required to register under EPFO. Voluntary registration is also available.
What is the ESI threshold?
ESIC applies to establishments with 10+ employees where wages are up to ₹21,000/month. ESI contribution is 0.75% (employee) + 3.25% (employer).
What happens if PF is not paid on time?
Late payment attracts damages (5–25% per annum) and interest (12% p.a.). Willful non-compliance can lead to prosecution.

FAQ
When is an E-Way Bill required?
E-Way Bill is required for movement of goods valued above ₹50,000 for inter-state transport. For intra-state, thresholds vary by state.
How long is an E-Way Bill valid?
Validity depends on distance: 1 day per 100 km for regular cargo. Can be extended if goods cannot reach destination within valid period.
Can E-Way Bills be cancelled?
Yes, within 24 hours of generation if movement has not begun. After 24 hours, cancellation is not possible electronically.
